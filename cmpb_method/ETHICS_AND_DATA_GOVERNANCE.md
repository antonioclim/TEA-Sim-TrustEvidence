# Ethics and data governance

The distributed examples and all events used by the integrated passage are synthetic. No person was recruited, no clinical intervention was performed and no identifiable or restricted patient record is included. On that basis, the software experiments do not constitute human-subject research in this package.

The earlier workload-design work reported an aggregate-only extraction from one openly available CGM participant file. This release does not redistribute that file, its samples or its participant identifier. The integrated workload is generated entirely from synthetic templates.

This statement is a software-package boundary, not legal advice or a determination by an ethics committee. Any future use with patient data, clinical systems or recruited participants requires independent governance, ethics and information-security review.
