# LJI status decision

## Decision

The Ledger Justification Index (LJI) remains an exploratory backend-selection heuristic and design-science decision artefact. It is not expert validated, not Delphi endorsed, not psychometrically validated and not legally or clinically validated.

## Basis

No institutional ethics approval, documented waiver, genuine anonymised expert data, completed two-round panel, response-rate record or consensus matrix was present in the corpus. Expert-validation material was protocol scaffolding only. Toy/sample Delphi files are useful only for script smoke testing and must not be treated as evidence.

## Allowed wording

"A structured expert-validation protocol is supplied for subsequent evaluation. The LJI remains an exploratory decision-support heuristic."

## Forbidden wording

Do not use: expert validated, Delphi consensus, RAND/UCLA consensus, expert-reviewed LJI, content validated, validated decision framework, expert-endorsed thresholds or generalisable expert agreement.
