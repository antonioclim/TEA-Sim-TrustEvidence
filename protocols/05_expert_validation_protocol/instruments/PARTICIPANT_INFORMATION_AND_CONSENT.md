# Participant information and consent form

You are invited to participate in a structured expert review of an information-systems research artefact for auditable health information exchange. Participation involves two questionnaire rounds, each using 1-9 ratings and optional comments.

Round 2 will show controlled anonymised feedback from Round 1 before you re-rate retained or revised items. The study does not ask you to provide patient data, clinical cases, legal advice, confidential employer information, system secrets or raw audit logs.

Participation is voluntary. You may decline any question. You may withdraw before anonymised aggregation according to the approved ethics procedure. Results will be reported in aggregate with anonymised thematic summaries.

By continuing, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old, understand the study information and consent to participate under the approved ethics procedure.
