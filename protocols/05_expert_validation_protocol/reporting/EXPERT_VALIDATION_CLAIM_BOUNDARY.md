# Expert-validation claim boundary

Allowed now: planned protocol, ethics-ready instruments, future content-validation pathway, exploratory LJI heuristic.

Forbidden now: expert validation, Delphi consensus, RAND/UCLA consensus, expert-reviewed LJI, content validity, expert-endorsed thresholds, generalisable expert support, clinical validation, legal compliance validation or certification.

The protocol may be cited in supplementary material only as a future validation instrument unless genuine data later satisfy the pre-specified criteria.
