# Ethics determination request template

## Project title

Structured expert review of the TrustEvidence boundary and backend proportionality logic for auditable health information systems.

## Requested determination

Before recruitment, the investigator requests institutional confirmation of approval, exemption, waiver or requirement for full review.

## Proposed risk level

Minimal risk, subject to institutional confirmation. The protocol solicits professional opinions about an information-systems artefact and does not solicit patient data, clinical decisions, legal advice, operational secrets or confidential employer information.

## Data to be collected

Professional stratum, years-of-experience band, conflict-of-interest declaration, 1-9 item ratings, optional free-text comments, consent status and withdrawal status.

## Data not to be collected

Patient records, identifiable patient data, raw clinical payloads, clinical case decisions, system credentials, security secrets, confidential incident data or legal advice.

## Data handling

Recruitment contact data must be stored separately from pseudonymised survey data. Publications should report aggregate item statistics and anonymised thematic summaries only.
