# Eligibility and conflict-of-interest controls

Participants must have at least five years of relevant professional or research experience, a doctoral-level publication record or a recognised implementation/standards/governance role in an eligible stratum.

Exclude direct artefact authors, dependent supervisees or employees of the investigator where independence is compromised, participants unable to provide consent and participants intending to disclose patient data, confidential employer data, system secrets or legal advice.

Record vendor, organisational, standards-body, consultancy, supervisory and authorship relationships. Report conflicts by category in aggregate.
